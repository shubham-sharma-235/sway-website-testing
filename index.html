Sway’s story

SWAY is a Jaipur-based costume and Adornment design studio that transforms fabric into storytelling. What began as a humble initiative to create personalized dance costumes has grown into a full-fledged creative atelier — crafting outfits for stage, screen, celebrations, and sacred rituals.

From the sparkle of a bride’s lehenga to the elegance of classical dancewear, SWAY believes that every piece of clothing should narrate a moment. Each piece is thoughtfully designed, stitched with purpose, and tailored to reflect the individuality of its wearer.

Our journey is rooted in heritage yet driven by a contemporary spirit — blending traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics to create meaningful, performance-ready attire that resonates across generations and cultures.

About Us

Crafted Stories in Every Stitch

From classical dance costumes to bespoke bridal attire, each creation at SWAY is designed with purpose — a delicate harmony of artistry, heritage, and contemporary design. We blend centuries-old craftsmanship with modern aesthetics to bring your vision to life, whether it’s for the stage, screen, or sacred rituals.

Every pleat, seam, and embellishment is thoughtfully considered, celebrating not just fabric and form, but the unique narrative of each wearer. At SWAY, we don’t just make garments; we craft living stories that move with you and for you.

Meet The Founder

Dr Swati Malik, Mr Vijay Solomon

I'm Diva, a developer and writer blending code and creativity. My journey into the world of travel started as a young adventurer with a relentless curiosity about the world. Over the years, this curiosity has transformed into a deep passion for exploring experiencing diverse cultures, and meeting incredible people along the way.

As a developer and writer, I bring a unique perspective to my travel adventures. My background in technology allows me to navigate the digital landscape effortlessly, while my love for storytelling helps me capture and share the essence of my journeys in a way that resonates with my readers. I believe that travel is more than just visiting new places; it’s about immersing oneself in the local culture, understanding different ways of life, and finding beauty in the unexpected.
