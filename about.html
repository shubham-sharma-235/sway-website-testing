About Sway

Sway’s story

SWAY is a Jaipur-based costume and Adornment design studio that transforms fabric into storytelling. What began as a humble initiative to create personalized dance costumes has grown into a full-fledged creative atelier — crafting outfits for stage, screen, celebrations, and sacred rituals.

From the sparkle of a bride’s lehenga to the elegance of classical dancewear, SWAY believes that every piece of clothing should narrate a moment. Each piece is thoughtfully designed, stitched with purpose, and tailored to reflect the individuality of its wearer.

Our journey is rooted in heritage yet driven by a contemporary spirit — blending traditional craftsmanship with modern aesthetics to create meaningful, performance-ready attire that resonates across generations and cultures.

Names of the founder
